"Bleach" is a Japanese anime and manga series created by Tite Kubo. It follows the story of Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager with the ability to see ghosts. His life takes a dramatic turn when he inadvertently acquires the powers of a Soul Reaper, a celestial being responsible for protecting souls and maintaining the balance between the living and the dead. Ichigo becomes embroiled in battles against evil spirits known as Hollows and uncovers a vast, interconnected spirit world. Known for its dynamic action sequences, complex characters, and intricate lore, "Bleach" has garnered a dedicated fan base over its run, becoming a significant part of the anime and manga landscape.
