"My Hero Academia" is an action-packed anime set in a world where nearly everyone possesses unique superpowers called Quirks. The story follows Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero. When he inherits a powerful Quirk from the legendary hero All Might, Izuku enrolls in U.A. High School to hone his abilities. Filled with thrilling battles, intense training, and a gripping hero-vs-villain dynamic, the series explores themes of heroism, perseverance, and self-discovery in a society where heroes and villains clash.
